Generates insights, suggestions, questions and references when you browse a paper on a scientific journal website like Arxiv directly from the abstract using ChatGPT. 48 websites are currently supported. For full list of supported websites check out our website(github). Feel free to send a pull request if you need for any other website. No ChatGPT Plus plan required.
